Privacy Policy – Pregnancy Test

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Pregnancy Test application (com.jacek.miszczyk.pregnancytestLite), developed by Jacek Miszczyk, handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal health information you enter (all quiz answers remain on your device and are never sent to any server).

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

  • Google AdMob – displays advertisements and may collect the Android Advertising ID and other device identifiers to serve relevant ads.
  • Google Firebase Analytics – collects anonymous usage statistics (e.g. app opens, crashes) to help improve the app.

2. Android Advertising ID

Google AdMob may use the Android Advertising ID (AAID) to provide personalised advertisements. You can reset or opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device settings:

Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+)
or
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation (older versions)

For more information see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How Information Is Used

  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics via Firebase Analytics.

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties beyond the services listed above.

4. Medical Disclaimer

This application is for entertainment and informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnoses. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding any health concerns.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
